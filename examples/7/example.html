Services

Strategy

The provision of a blue-print for your email marketing growth strategy.
‍
Looking to make a big impact with your email campaigns? Our email marketing strategy service is the perfect starting point. We'll work with you to develop a unique email marketing plan that aligns with your business goals and target audience. From crafting a compelling email marketing message to developing a robust email marketing schedule, we've got you covered.

Design

The conceptualization and design execution of all email marketing designs.
‍
Don't let boring email templates hold you back from engaging with your customers. Our email marketing design service will help you create visually stunning emails that capture your brand's essence and resonate with your audience. Our team of designers is well-versed in the latest design trends and can help you create custom email templates that will make your emails stand out from the crowd.

Copywriting

The provision of all written copy based on the brand tone and voice.
‍
‍Words matter, especially when it comes to email marketing. Our email marketing copywriting service will help you create engaging email content that speaks to your audience and drives results. We'll work with you to craft compelling email subject lines, body copy, and calls-to-action that encourage your customers to take action.

Automation

The automation of all email marketing flows based on user behaviour.

Save time and increase efficiency with our email marketing automation service. We'll help you set up automated email campaigns that reach your customers at the right time with the right message. Whether you're looking to nurture leads, welcome new subscribers, or re-engage inactive customers, our email marketing automation service will help you achieve your goals.

Optimization

The quarterly optimization of the entire email marketing strategy.
‍
Looking to improve your email marketing performance? Our email marketing optimization service will help you fine-tune your campaigns for maximum impact. We'll analyze your email marketing data and provide insights on how to improve your open rates, click-through rates, and overall ROI. From A/B testing subject lines to optimizing your email design, we'll help you achieve your email marketing goals.

Analytics

The provision of monthly analytical reports based on email marketing performance.

‍Data is king when it comes to email marketing, and our email marketing analytics service will help you make sense of your email marketing data. We'll provide you with detailed reports on your email campaign performance, including open rates, click-through rates, conversion rates, and more. With our email marketing analytics service, you'll have the insights you need to make data-driven decisions and optimize your email marketing strategy.
